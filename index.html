Welcome to BRM Contractors Inc. We are a family owned and operated contracting business located in the heart of Pennsylvania. We specialize in all aspects of residential and commercial construction services. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to delivering superior results to all of our projects. We take pride in our work and strive to exceed expectations. At BRM Contractors Inc, we understand that a construction project can be daunting and requires knowledge, skill and attention to detail. We use the latest technology, tools and resources to get the job done right the first time. We are experienced in all areas of construction, from foundation and framing to siding and roofing. We also offer services such as remodeling, additions and custom carpentry. If you need any type of residential or commercial building service, look no further than BRM Contractors Inc. We are here to help make your dreams come to life, so give us a call today to get started.
